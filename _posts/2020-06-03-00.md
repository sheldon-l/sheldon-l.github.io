## Main Notations

- Major = Maj = Ma = M = maj = $\Delta$ = (#, somtimes)
- minor = min = mi = m = (b, somtimes)
- diminished = dimin = dim = $^o$ = (b, somtimes)
- augmented = aug = $^+$ = (#, somtimes)
